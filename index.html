Our mission

Spotify has over 50 million tracks, with that much music it's difficult to know where to even start. That's why we created Spotify Song Suggester, we take the songs and artitst that you love and suggest similar music based off of that. This way you can keep music fresh by exploring the many artitsts and songs that are out there.

The engineering behind it

With the combined efforts of our data science and web development team, you can now find songs by their specfic qualities not just their genre.
